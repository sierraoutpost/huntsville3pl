### 1.2 Economic drivers

1. **Aerospace & defense** — NASA Marshall Space Flight Center, U.S. Space Command (permanently relocated to Redstone Arsenal), Missile Defense Agency, Army Materiel Command, Space & Missile Defense Command. Boeing, Lockheed Martin, Raytheon, Northrop Grumman all maintain major operations.
2. **Technology & engineering** — Cummings Research Park (2nd largest research park in the U.S., 300+ companies). The metro has nearly 80,000 workers in aerospace-related employment alone. Growing software, cybersecurity, and AI sectors.
3. **Advanced manufacturing** — Automotive (Mazda-Toyota joint venture in nearby Limestone County), rocket propulsion manufacturing, precision machining, defense hardware production.
4. **Federal government & military** — Redstone Arsenal is the anchor, hosting Army commands and approximately 46,000 military, civilian, and contractor personnel. U.S. Space Command HQ brings ~1,400 additional positions.
5. **Biotech & healthcare** — HudsonAlpha Institute for Biotechnology, Huntsville Hospital (one of the largest in Alabama), growing life sciences corridor.

## 2. Logistics Significance

### 2.1 Key interstate corridors

| Interstate | Connects to | Significance |
|---|---|---|
| **I-65** | Birmingham (south) ↔ Nashville (north) ↔ Louisville ↔ Indianapolis ↔ Chicago | Primary north-south freight corridor; connects Huntsville to the Gulf Coast (Mobile) and the Midwest. Major national freight artery. |
| **I-565** | Huntsville ↔ I-65 interchange (Decatur area) | Bisects Huntsville east-west; provides direct, uncongested access from the Port of Huntsville and industrial parks to I-65. |
| **US-72** | Chattanooga (east) ↔ Huntsville ↔ Florence/Muscle Shoals (west) | East-west corridor linking Huntsville to Chattanooga (another Sams 3PL city) and the Shoals region; serves as a secondary freight route. |

### 2.2 Port / airport / rail infrastructure

| Facility | Type | Key data |
|---|---|---|
| **Huntsville International Airport (HSV)** | Cargo airport | 88,769 metric tons of cargo (2019); ranked 21st in continental U.S. for international air cargo; 7 major cargo airlines; parallel 12,600-ft and 10,000-ft runways; $230M invested in air cargo infrastructure. Voted "Best Small Airport" in 2025 Newsweek Readers' Choice Awards. One of 20 FAA-licensed spaceports nationwide. |
| **International Intermodal Center (IIC)** | Inland port / intermodal | Premier inland port of entry for international air cargo and ocean containers by rail. 33,745 rail lifts in 2024 (22.3% YoY growth). Capacity: 1,100 wheeled units, 500+ stacking loads, two 45-ton gantry cranes. $70M+ invested in rail intermodal infrastructure. |
| **Norfolk Southern main line** | Class I railroad | Connected via 5-mile rail spur to IIC; direct access to major East and West Coast ports. |
| **CSX interchange** | Class I railroad | Via Huntsville & Madison County Railroad Authority shortline (14 miles of track), interchange at Tanner, AL. |
| **Port of Decatur** | Inland waterway port | 30 minutes from Huntsville; one of the busiest ports on the Tennessee River; access to Tennessee-Tombigbee Waterway; served by Norfolk Southern and CSX; barge facilities linking to Gulf of Mexico. |

**Source(s):**

### 2.3 Border crossings (if applicable)

N/A — no international border crossings. However, the International Intermodal Center serves as an inland port of entry for international cargo via air and rail, providing customs clearance capabilities without a physical border crossing.

### 2.4 One-day drive reach

> Within a one-day drive (~500 miles) from Huntsville: Nashville, TN (110 mi / ~1.5 hr), Birmingham, AL (100 mi / ~1.5 hr), Atlanta, GA (190 mi / ~3 hr), Memphis, TN (220 mi / ~3.5 hr), Chattanooga, TN (110 mi / ~2 hr), Louisville, KY (300 mi / ~4.5 hr), Jackson, MS (280 mi / ~4 hr), Knoxville, TN (210 mi / ~3 hr), Mobile, AL (350 mi / ~5 hr), New Orleans, LA (440 mi / ~6 hr), Charlotte, NC (400 mi / ~5.5 hr), Indianapolis, IN (380 mi / ~5.5 hr), St. Louis, MO (420 mi / ~6 hr). Huntsville reaches the majority of the Southeast's population and freight markets within a single day's drive, with next-day LTL service available to all of these markets.
